CornerStone



Cornerstone is a great program especially if you have any mental disabilities and differences. They work extremely hard to accomodate everyone and help them all succeed. They offer services for most of the core curriculum at WashU. This includes the Writing Help Desk, the Physics Help Desk, and the Calc Help Desk. These desks provide WashU students the opportunity to review course material and get extra help in the subjects. Each of these do not require appointments in order to be helped. In addition to these services, Cornerstone also hosts a weekly physics help session in which students work together with an upperclassmen to solve the weekly physics problem set. Futhermore, Cornerstone will assist students who need extra help by finding them a tutor to help them improve in the subject they please. Cornerstone has also established study groups called of 7 to 8 students in subjects such as chemistry and calculus. These study groups meet once a week to review the material from the previous week with a TA. The students lead the discussion of the topic and work on an additional problem set. The TA is there to steer them onto the right track in finding the correct answers, but they do not supply the answers to the problem. This technique allows the students to work through the material and make new connections themselves, which they are more likely to remember later. Cornerstone goes above and beyond to support the students of WashU academically!

Student Technology Services



Student Technology Services have representatives that work for each individual residential college. They also have a walk in location beneath Gregg House on the South Forty. This is a student run organization that provides computer troubleshootin services to WashU Students.

What Can You Do At STS?

  • Get Computer Hardware Problems Fixed!
  • Get Computer Software Problems Fixed!
  • Get General Computer Opperation Questions Answered!
  • Get Advice About Which Computer Technology To Purchase!
  • Numerous Other Things!
